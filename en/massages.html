Traditional Thai Massage

Traditional Thai massage (Thai: Nuad Phaen Boran), also known as Thai Yoga massage, is a unique massage technique. It effectively combines elements of acupressure, passive yoga (stretching), meditation, and energy work. The focus is never on just one area of the body. As with other Far Eastern treatment methods, TTM always considers the person as a whole.

Thai Massage with Oil and Hot-Stone

Thai massage with oil combines the typical massage techniques of traditional Thai massage (Nuad Thai) with the additional use of massage oil. It is gentler and includes fewer full-body stretches. Massage oil is used to allow hands to glide better over the skin and prevent skin irritation from strong friction.
